Lion Shark

Description:

New and beautiful hybrid from Space Chilli Peppers between Carolina Reaper Red and Pimenta Da Neyde.
Spontaneous mutation to peach color that occurred in a Red Shark F2 plant.
Very productive plants, they have been selected with sun with dark leaves for the selection of seeds.
Fruits ripen in black / purple to peach clusters upon completion.
High spiciness, but not intrusive, very fruity,
this strain has inherited a lot of strength from the Reaper and growing it gives great satisfaction!
Seeds not isolated.
