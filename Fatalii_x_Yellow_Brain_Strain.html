Fatalii x Yellow Brain Strain

Description:

This is one of my favorites. Has a great citrus flavor and is very hot. They don't look like much, but the heat is was very impressive, at least a split between the Fatalii and Yellow Brain Strain if not leaning more towards the brain strain heat. I got the seeds for these from my friend Jeff Hobbs, who told he he received his from our friend Dave Schram who I believe is the creator of this strain. The filial generation of these is unknown and therefore must be considered unstable. Your grow results could vary from this description.
