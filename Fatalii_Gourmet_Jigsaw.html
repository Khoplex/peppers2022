Fatalii Gourmet Jigsaw

Description:

The Hippy Seed Company

The Fatalii Gourmet Jigsaw is an insane creation from the Finish grower Jukka. He crossed the Trinidad Moruga Scorpion with a secret variety years ago.

The Fatalii Gourmet Jigsaw chili is insanely HOT and we even find it hotter than the Carolina Reaper. It has a different kind of heat where you will experience a numbness of your tongue. The heat keeps on building and it is a beast of a chili. We also have the Chocolate Jigsaw, if you love chocolate colored pods.

In a pot the plant grows to over a meter in height and is looks amazing with these fiery red chili peppers.

You can use the Jigsaw for a hot sauce or a super charged chili powder.
